History of Paul Allen

He was born in 1953 in Seattle, Washington. He attended Lakeside School which is a private school and he met Bill Gates there. In 1975 he and Bill Gates founded Microsoft. He left Microsoft in 1982 because he was diagnosed with Hodgkin's lymphoma and disagreements with Bill Gates. He was stil on the board of directors and became friends with Bill Gates again in 1986. He fully left Microsoft in 2000 but still had shares in the company.
